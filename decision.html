Given the growing demand for housing, particularly in a desirable coastal location like Babbacombe, the introduction of modern residential properties was seen as a practical and necessary step forward.

Strict guidelines were put in place regarding the design and materials to be used, ensuring that the finished buildings would be of high quality and visually complementary to their surroundings. Additionally, measures were introduced to mitigate the impact of construction on local residents.
